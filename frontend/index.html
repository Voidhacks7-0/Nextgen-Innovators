Welcome to SVVV College Indore

Shri Vaishnav Vidyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya is a private university in Indore known for academic excellence, vibrant campus life, innovation, research, and industry-oriented programs.

About SVVV

SVVV was established with the vision to transform society through value-based education. The university offers engineering, management, sciences, humanities, and professional courses supported by modern infrastructure and highly experienced faculty. SVVV focuses on holistic development, research, skill building, and character formation.
